# Lavrion Study Area Map

Interactive map for soil contamination citizen science project in Lavrion, Greece.

**View the map:** https://jangelamaya.github.io/Lavrion-miningwaste-map/

# Source: Demetriades et al., 1999d: 2.3, Map 2.3
Demetriades, A., Vergou-Vichou, K, Stavrakis, P. and Vassiliades, E., 1999d. General thematic maps of the Lavrion urban environment, in A. Demetriades (Editor), Geochemical atlas of the Lavrion urban area for environmental protection and planning; Volume 2 (ed. A. Demetriades), Project ‘Soil rehabilitation in the Municipality of Lavrion’, EU LIFE programme Contract No. 93/GR/A14/GR/4576, Institute of Geology and Mineral Exploration, Athens, Hellas, Open File Report E8272, Chapter 2, pp. 2.1–2.16.

## Instructions
- Toggle between study area map and satellite imagery using checkboxes
- Click and drag to pan, scroll to zoom
- Mouse over the map to see coordinates
